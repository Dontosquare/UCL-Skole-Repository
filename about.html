About me

My name is Joachim, i am 25 years old and studying in Odense to become a multimediadesigner. I have a big passion for creative projects and i love a good challenge where you need to give your everything.

I have worked with creative projects since the age of 12, when i first got my hands on moviemaker, although what i made is now lost media. in 2014 started producing edm music and in 2016 make music “visualisers” on youtube.

When i am not deep in coding or one of the design tools, i love to watch formula 1 and football, hang out with friends or just chill out on the couch playing some playstation. Like mentioned before, i also produce edm music under the artist name Martin Lübeck.

My goals

Its always healthy to have ambitious career wishes. I dream about being a part of the “maang-soup” which is the inside joke about Meta, Apple, Amazon, Netflix and Google.

I love the work enviroment, and there are videoes on youtube that shows the days in the life of a developer. although i know they are very romanticised, and the job itself will be very tough, i still feel like i belong there. I have friends who became senior developers at some of the big clothing e-shops here in Denmark, they are both my mentors and my inspiration.

I love frontend coding, and i already am coding concepts for small businesses around town. Although its just passion projects and not real clients. If i want to progress down the line, i could see myself as a full stack developer

My abilities

As a multimediadesigner, i can help with your projects on a number of differnet branches and areas. I can do html and css coding, i can create content like commercial shoots and product pictures or help your team with research, where i both do quality and quantity reserach. You just tell me where you need my assistance and i will happily look into it.
